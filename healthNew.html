This application is a health app and is mainly focused on people who are willing to change their current lifestyle to a healthy lifestyle. The most common functionality is where the individual user would log in into the app and then estimate how his current status of health is. Depending on it the user would go for a run and the app would count his steps, give him feedback on his calories and days remaining to reach the goal.

Ideation & Strategy

During my initial analysis, I tried to look at similar apps, I did realize that it isn't a new concept yet I was unsatisfied with the current market offerings. Most of them needed to enable network services to track steps which would result in lower battery power for the user.

Hence came to the idea of step tracking without using the network but onboard sensors to compute data. Moreover, I wanted to allow the user to keep track of the food items along with the calories he has consumed. The order of activities can be seen here.

Challenge

The question was
"How to calculate steps using accelerometer rather than using location , when no inbuilt pedometer sensors are available ? ""

Solution

The solution is a follows :
1) Using inbuilt library (TYPE_STEP_DETECTOR), (TYPE_STEP_COUNTER)
2) The way it works is that it calculates average velocity by guessing where the global Z vector is. This is done by calculating the X, Y, Z values of the accelerometer. The average of 50 readings is taken for all of them.
3) It is then multiplied with a step delay of 250000000 Sec. All this is then converted into individual steps being counted.
